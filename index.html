Diocletian

Roman emperor // last official Roman persecutor of Christianity

December 22nd, 244 AD - December 3rd, 311 AD

Wikipedia »

Front (obverse)

Reference image

Legend

IMP(erator) C(aesar) C(aius) VAL(erius) DIOCLETIANVS P(ius) F(elix) AVG(ustus)

"The pious, happy and sublime Emperor Caesar Gaius Valerius Diocletianus"

Imagery

Bust of Diocletian facing right, wearing a so-called radiate crown and a military cloak.

Back (reverse)

Reference image

Legend

CONCORDIA MILITVM

"Unity of the army"

Imagery

The god Jupiter, standing on the left and holding a sceptre, is handing over a personification of Victory (the tiny figure in between the larger ones) to Diocletian, who is standing on the right and is holding a fancy sceptre called a parazonium.
